# Visual Direction Brief

## Product Character
- Brand feeling: warm guidance with calm utility
- Trust posture: humane, competent, discreet
- Energy level: low-friction and present, never noisy
- Keywords: companion, reflection, continuity, confidence

## Visual Thesis
- Personality should feel like a thoughtful personal workspace, not a cold
  admin panel or sci-fi robot console.
- When the product needs a stronger signature motif, prefer a humane symbolic
  embodiment of cognition over abstract dashboard ornament.

## Surface Notes
- Mobile: conversation-first, reachable controls, emotional clarity
- Tablet: balanced split between transcript and contextual tools
- Desktop: focused chat plus supportive side panels for memory, tools, and
  settings

## Signature Motif
- Approved motif:
  a warm synthetic human-like figure that maps AION concepts through anchored
  pins, held symbolic objects, and timeline layers
- Use cases:
  public entry trust framing, personality overview, tools capability framing,
  and lightweight chat/settings support illustrations
- Constraints:
  keep the motif symbolic and editorial
  keep actions and content more prominent than the illustration
  keep the same grammar across desktop, tablet, and mobile

## Explicit Avoids
- robotic neon assistant aesthetics
- cluttered chatbot layouts
- sterile enterprise settings screens
- mascot art that has no route-level information value
