# Experience Quality Bar

- Make the next action feel obvious and safe within the first viewport.
- Keep conversation, settings, and tools surfaces emotionally clear and easy to
  trust.
- Design loading, empty, error, and success states as real product moments.
- Mobile should optimize message flow, settings confidence, and quick recovery.
- Tablet should add browsing and comparison comfort, not just scale up mobile.
- Desktop should support transcript continuity and visible context without
  panel clutter.
- Motion should orient the user and reinforce trust, never perform for its own
  sake.
